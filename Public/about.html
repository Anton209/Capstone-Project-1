About Me:

Hello! I'm Anthony Lyons, originally from East New Britain Province in Papua New Guinea. I have Papua New Guinean and English heritage, which has given me a rich multicultural perspective.

I spent most of my junior school years in Rabaul before moving to Australia to attend Toowoomba Grammar School as a boarding student. During this time, I actively participated in sports such as volleyball, soccer, and rugby, which helped me develop teamwork, discipline, and leadership skills.

After high school, I studied nursing at the University of Sunshine Coast for a year. Although I found the subject interesting, I realized it wasn't the right fit for me and decided to return home to assist with my family's company. This experience has strengthened my work ethic and sense of community.

In addition to my academic and work experiences, I volunteered at Sony Camp, caring for children with special needs. This taught me patience, compassion, and the importance of giving back.

I'm passionate about personal growth, helping others, and making a positive impact in my community. Looking ahead, I aim to continue developing my skills and exploring new opportunities that align with my values and interests.
